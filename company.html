Ketan Corporation (Hindalco - Authorised Stockist)

M/s Ketan Corporation was established in the year 1976.

Ketan Corporation is Authorised Stockist for Flat Rolled Products for M/S. HINDALCO Industries Ltd. Hindalco’s aluminium rolled products is a prime source for all major brands and are used in a multitude of industrial segments.

We are the leading trader, wholesaler, distributor and supplier of Aluminum Sheet, Aluminum Coil, Aluminium Plate, Aluminum Flooring Sheets, Aluminium Checkered Sheet , Pure Aluminum Circles, Aluminium Stucco Coil, Aluminium Extrusions, Aluminium Heat Sinks etc.

We are in constant touch with different industries in order to understand the specifications and needs of our customers. With our wide network and an excellent infrastructure we can fulfill customer requirements. We act as a liaison between Manufacturers and Suppliers in order to serve our customer satisfactorily. We can supply aluminium material in any non-standard length and width as required by the customers.

Our goal is to maintain and build customer relation by providing world class customer service by delivering Quality goods in Time. We work with our customer's schedules and requirements and work hand in glove with their accurate planning. With products and services meeting and even exceeding customer expectations, the company continues to grow and spread its wings.

Ensuring of great quality with the delivery of best service by which we would not hesitate to tell that Quality is never an accident; it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, intelligent direction and skilful execution; it represents the wise choice of many alternatives.

We have more than 40 years of vast experience in the field of Aluminium and during this tenure we have garnered a wide client Base.

Our fair pricing policies coupled with customer friendly payment modes have made us the most preferred supplier.

Under the direction of our mentor “Lalbhai Shah”, we have achieved a valuable place in this industry.

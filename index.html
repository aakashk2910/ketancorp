ABOUT OUR COMPANY

M/s Ketan Corporation was established in the year 1976. Ketan Corporation is Authorised Stockist for Flat Rolled Products for M/S. HINDALCO Industries Ltd. Hindalco’s aluminium rolled products is a prime source for all major brands and are used in a multitude of industrial segments. We are the leading trader, wholesaler, distributor and supplier of Aluminum Sheet, Aluminum Coil, Aluminium Plate, Aluminum Flooring Sheets, Aluminium Checkered Sheet , Pure Aluminum Circles, Aluminium Stucco Coil, Aluminium Extrusions, Aluminium Heat Sinks etc.We are in constant touch with different industries in order to understand the specifications and needs of our customers. With our wide network and an excellent infrastructure we can fulfill customer requirements. We act as a liaison between Manufacturers and Suppliers in order to serve our customer satisfactorily. We can supply aluminium material in any non-standard length and width as required by the customers.
